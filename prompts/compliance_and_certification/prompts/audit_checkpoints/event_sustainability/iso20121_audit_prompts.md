## Context and Background

### ISO 20121:2012 Event Sustainability Management Systems - Requirements with Guidance for Use
**Effective Date**: June 1, 2012  
**Scope**: Organizations involved in event planning and delivery seeking sustainable event management  
**Key Principles**: Holistic approach, stakeholder engagement, life cycle thinking, continual improvement  
**Structure**: Requirements for sustainable event management systems  
**Benefits**: Reduced environmental impact, improved stakeholder relationships, enhanced reputation, cost savings

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 20121**: International standard for sustainable event management
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides framework for sustainable event practices
- **Global Application**: Applicable to events of all sizes and types
- **Integration**: Compatible with other management system standards
