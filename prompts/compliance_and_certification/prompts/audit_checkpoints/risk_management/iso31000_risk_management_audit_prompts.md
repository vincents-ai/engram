# ISO 31000 Risk Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 31000:2018 Risk Management implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 31000:2018 Risk Management - Guidelines
**Effective Date**: February 15, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Risk management framework applicable to any organization  
**Key Principles**: Integrated, structured, comprehensive, customized, inclusive, dynamic, best available information, human factors, continual improvement  
**Structure**: Guidelines covering risk management framework, process, and integration  
**Benefits**: Improved decision making, enhanced organizational resilience, better resource allocation, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 31000**: International standard for risk management
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides guidance rather than requirements
- **Integration**: Applicable to all management system standards
- **Global Application**: Suitable for organizations of all sizes and sectors

## ISO 31000 Risk Assessment Framework

**Risk Categories:**
- **Strategic Risks**: Market changes, competition, regulatory changes
- **Operational Risks**: Process failures, human error, technology failures
- **Financial Risks**: Credit, liquidity, market, currency risks
- **Compliance Risks**: Legal, regulatory, contractual non-compliance
- **Reputational Risks**: Brand damage, stakeholder trust loss

**Risk Analysis Methods:**
- **Qualitative**: Risk matrices, expert judgment, scenario analysis
- **Quantitative**: Statistical analysis, Monte Carlo simulation, financial modeling
- **Semi-Quantitative**: Combination of qualitative and quantitative approaches
