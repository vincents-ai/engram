This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for key gaming jurisdictions: Gibraltar GRA, Alderney AGCC, Isle of Man GSC, Curaçao eGaming, and US States.

## Context and Background

### Gibraltar GRA (Gambling Regulation Authority)
**License Type**: Remote Gambling License (Class 1, 2, 3, 4)  
**Regulatory Framework**: Gambling Act 2017, EU-aligned regulations  
**Key Requirements**: Technical standards, player protection, AML compliance  
**Benefits**: EU market access, established jurisdiction, strong regulatory oversight

### Alderney AGCC (Alderney Gambling Control Commission)
**License Categories**: Category 1 (general), Category 2 (restricted)  
**Regulatory Approach**: Principles-based regulation, international standards  
**Key Requirements**: Corporate governance, risk management, player safeguards  
**Benefits**: Tax-efficient, reputable jurisdiction, flexible licensing

### Isle of Man GSC (Gambling Supervision Commission)
**License Type**: Online Gambling License  
**Regulatory Framework**: Gambling Act 2015, risk-based supervision  
**Key Requirements**: Technical compliance, responsible gambling, AML controls  
**Benefits**: Established jurisdiction, strong regulatory framework, innovation-friendly

### Curaçao eGaming
**License Type**: Master License with sub-licenses  
**Regulatory Framework**: National Decree on Lotteries 1993  
**Key Requirements**: GLI certification, server location, player protection  
**Benefits**: Cost-effective licensing, international acceptance, flexible structure

### US State Gaming Jurisdictions
**License Types**: Varies by state (casino, sports betting, iGaming)  
**Regulatory Framework**: State-specific gaming control acts  
**Key Requirements**: GLI standards, responsible gambling, tribal compacts  
**Benefits**: Large market access, established regulatory frameworks, consumer protection

## Regulatory Context
- **European Jurisdictions**: EU-aligned regulations, strong player protection focus
- **Curaçao**: Popular for international operators, cost-effective licensing
- **US States**: State-by-state regulation, growing sports betting and iGaming markets
- **Global Standards**: GLI technical standards, responsible gambling requirements
