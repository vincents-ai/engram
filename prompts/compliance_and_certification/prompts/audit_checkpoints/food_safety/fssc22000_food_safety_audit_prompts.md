# FSSC 22000 Food Safety Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for FSSC 22000 Food Safety System Certification implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### FSSC 22000: Food Safety System Certification
**Effective Date**: 2009 (current version 6.1)  
**Scope**: Food manufacturers and organizations in the food supply chain  
**Key Principles**: HACCP, prerequisite programs, food safety management, continuous improvement  
**Structure**: Requirements combining ISO 22000, ISO/TS 22002, and additional FSSC requirements  
**Benefits**: Enhanced food safety, regulatory compliance, consumer confidence, market access

## Regulatory Context
- **FSSC 22000**: Global food safety certification scheme
- **GFSI Recognition**: Recognized by Global Food Safety Initiative
- **Supply Chain Coverage**: Applicable throughout the food supply chain
- **International Acceptance**: Accepted worldwide for food safety assurance

## FSSC 22000 Food Safety Assessment

**Food Safety Management Components:**
- **HACCP Plan**: Hazard analysis and critical control points
- **Prerequisite Programs**: Basic hygiene and operational conditions
- **Traceability System**: Product tracking from farm to fork
- **Supplier Management**: Supplier qualification and monitoring

**Critical Control Points:**
- **Receiving**: Raw material acceptance criteria
- **Storage**: Temperature and humidity control
- **Processing**: Cooking, cooling, and processing controls
- **Packaging**: Packaging integrity and labeling
- **Distribution**: Transportation and storage controls
