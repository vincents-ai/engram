# ISO 22000 Food Safety Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 22000:2018 Food Safety Management System implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 22000:2018 Food Safety Management System
**Effective Date**: June 19, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations in the food chain seeking to implement HACCP and food safety management  
**Key Principles**: Interactive communication, system management, prerequisite programs, HACCP principles  
**Structure**: 10 clauses covering Context, Leadership, Planning, Support, Operation, Performance Evaluation, Improvement  
**Benefits**: Enhanced food safety, regulatory compliance, consumer confidence, reduced foodborne illness risks

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 22000**: International standard for food safety management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **HACCP Integration**: Incorporates HACCP principles as core requirement
- **Global Application**: Applicable throughout the food supply chain

**ISO 22000 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Context of the Organization (Clause 4)**
   - Evaluate internal/external issue identification
   - Assess interested party needs and expectations
   - Review food safety management system scope
   - Validate FSMS planning and objectives

2. **Leadership (Clause 5)**
   - Assess leadership and commitment to FSMS
   - Review food safety policy establishment
   - Validate organizational roles and responsibilities
   - Confirm planning for FSMS changes

3. **Planning (Clause 6)**
   - Evaluate hazards and prerequisite programs
   - Assess HACCP plan development
   - Review planning for FSMS changes
   - Validate planning of changes

4. **Support (Clause 7)**
   - Assess resource provision for FSMS
   - Review work environment management
   - Validate monitoring and measuring resources
   - Confirm organizational knowledge management

5. **Operation (Clause 8)**
   - Evaluate operational prerequisite programs
   - Assess HACCP plan implementation
   - Review traceability system effectiveness
   - Validate emergency preparedness

6. **Performance Evaluation (Clause 9)**
   - Assess monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation
   - Review compliance evaluation processes
   - Validate internal audit processes
   - Confirm management review processes

7. **Improvement (Clause 10)**
   - Evaluate improvement opportunities
   - Assess nonconformity and corrective action
   - Review continual improvement processes
   - Validate preventive action mechanisms
