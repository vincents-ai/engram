# ISO 39001 Road Traffic Safety Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 39001:2012 Road Traffic Safety Management implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 39001:2012 Road Traffic Safety (RTS) Management Systems - Requirements with Guidance for Use
**Effective Date**: October 15, 2012 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to improve road traffic safety performance  
**Key Principles**: Leadership, risk-based approach, stakeholder involvement, continual improvement  
**Structure**: Requirements covering RTS policy, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, improvement  
**Benefits**: Reduced road traffic incidents, improved safety culture, regulatory compliance, enhanced reputation

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 39001**: International standard for road traffic safety management
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides framework for RTS management
- **Global Application**: Applicable to organizations with road transport activities
- **Safety Focus**: Prevention of road traffic accidents and injuries

   - Review RTS management system scope

   - Assess leadership commitment to road safety
   - Review road traffic safety policy establishment
   - Validate organizational safety roles
   - Confirm RTS planning

3. **Planning**
   - Evaluate road traffic risk assessment
   - Assess RTS objective setting
   - Review RTS action planning
   - Validate planning of changes

4. **Support**
   - Assess RTS resource provision
   - Review RTS awareness training
   - Validate RTS communication
   - Confirm documented information management

5. **Operation - Road Traffic Risk Management**
   - Evaluate road traffic risk identification
   - Assess road traffic risk analysis and evaluation
   - Review road traffic risk treatment
   - Validate road traffic risk monitoring

6. **Operation - Safe Road Use**
   - Assess driver safety training programs
   - Review vehicle safety maintenance
   - Validate journey management procedures
   - Confirm emergency response planning

7. **Operation - Road Traffic Safety Culture**
   - Evaluate safety culture development
   - Assess safety awareness programs
   - Review safety behavior monitoring
   - Validate safety communication effectiveness

8. **Performance Evaluation**

## ISO 39001 Road Traffic Safety Assessment

**Road Traffic Risk Factors:**
- **Driver Factors**: Fatigue, distraction, impairment, training
- **Vehicle Factors**: Maintenance, condition, safety features
- **Road Factors**: Conditions, signage, infrastructure
- **Environmental Factors**: Weather, visibility, traffic conditions
- **Organizational Factors**: Policies, procedures, culture

**Safety Performance Metrics:**
- **Incident Rates**: Accidents per million kilometers
- **Severity Rates**: Injuries and fatalities per incident
- **Compliance Rates**: Policy adherence and training completion
- **Improvement Rates**: Year-over-year safety performance changes
