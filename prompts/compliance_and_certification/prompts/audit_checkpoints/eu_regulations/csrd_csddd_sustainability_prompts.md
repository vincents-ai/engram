**CSRD Implementation Roadmap:**

**Phase 1: Large Public Interest Entities (2024 Reporting)**
- Companies already subject to NFRD
- >500 employees threshold
- Listed companies, banks, insurance companies

**Phase 2: All Large Undertakings (2025 Reporting)**
- Companies meeting 2 of 3 criteria:
  - >250 employees
  - >€40 million revenue
  - >€20 million balance sheet total

**Phase 3: Listed SMEs (2026 Reporting)**
- Small and medium enterprises listed on regulated markets
- Simplified reporting standards available
- Opt-out possibility until 2028

**Phase 4: Non-EU Companies (2028 Reporting)**
- Large non-EU companies with significant EU activity
- >€150 million revenue in EU
- EU subsidiary or branch requirements
