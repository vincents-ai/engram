# EU Audit Directive Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for EU Audit Directive compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### EU Audit Directive (2014/56/EU)
**Effective Date**: June 17, 2014 (amended by 2014/95/EU)  
**Scope**: Statutory audits of public interest entities (PIEs) across EU member states  
**Key Requirements**: Audit quality, auditor independence, public oversight, transparency  
**Implementation**: National transposition required by member states  
**Penalties**: Administrative sanctions, fines, disqualification from audit services

## Regulatory Context
- **Directive 2014/56/EU**: Reforms statutory audit market to enhance quality and independence
- **Public Interest Entities**: Banks, insurance companies, listed companies
- **Audit Committee**: Independent oversight of audit process
- **Quality Assurance**: Peer reviews, inspections, continuing education requirements
