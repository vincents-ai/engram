# ISO 27701 Privacy Information Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 27701:2019 Privacy Information Management implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 27701:2019 Security Techniques - Extension to ISO 27001 and ISO 27002 for Privacy Information Management
**Effective Date**: June 15, 2019 (current version)  
**Scope**: Privacy information management for organizations acting as PII controllers or processors  
**Key Requirements**: PII identification, consent management, privacy impact assessment, breach management, privacy by design  
**Structure**: Extension to ISO 27001 covering privacy-specific controls and requirements  
**Benefits**: Enhanced privacy compliance, better PII protection, regulatory compliance, stakeholder trust

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 27701**: Extension of ISO 27001 for privacy management
- **PII Focus**: Personally identifiable information protection
- **Controller/Processor Roles**: Different requirements based on role
- **Privacy by Design**: Proactive privacy protection approach

## ISO 27701 Privacy Control Assessment

**Privacy-Specific Controls:**
- **PII Inventory**: Identification and classification of PII
- **Consent Management**: Consent collection, withdrawal, documentation
- **Privacy Impact Assessment**: PIA process and documentation
- **Data Subject Rights**: Access, rectification, erasure, portability
- **Breach Notification**: Detection, assessment, notification procedures
- **Privacy by Design**: Proactive privacy protection in systems
- **Vendor Management**: Privacy requirements in contracts
- **Training and Awareness**: Privacy training programs

**PII Protection Measures:**
- **Data Minimization**: Collection limitation, retention policies
- **Purpose Limitation**: Use restrictions, purpose specification
- **Lawful Basis**: Legal grounds for processing
- **Data Subject Rights**: Individual rights implementation
- **Security Measures**: Technical and organizational controls
- **International Transfers**: Cross-border transfer safeguards
