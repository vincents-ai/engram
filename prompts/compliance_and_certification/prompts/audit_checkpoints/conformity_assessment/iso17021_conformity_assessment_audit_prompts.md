# ISO 17021 Conformity Assessment Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 17021:2015 Conformity Assessment implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 17021:2015 Conformity Assessment - Requirements for Bodies Providing Audit and Certification of Management Systems
**Effective Date**: March 15, 2015 (current version)  
**Scope**: Certification bodies providing audit and certification services for management systems  
**Key Principles**: Impartiality, competence, responsibility, open-mindedness, consistency, effectiveness, risk-based approach  
**Structure**: Requirements covering certification body management, resource requirements, information requirements, certification processes  
**Benefits**: Consistent certification practices, enhanced credibility, improved stakeholder confidence, regulatory compliance

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 17021**: International standard for certification bodies
- **Accreditation Requirement**: Certification bodies must be accredited to ISO 17021
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for management system certification
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensures consistent and reliable certification services

1. **General Requirements**
   - Evaluate certification body legal entity status
   - Assess impartiality and independence
   - Review liability and financing adequacy
   - Validate management system implementation

   - Assess organizational structure appropriateness
   - Review governance and decision-making processes
   - Validate committee composition and operation
   - Confirm appeals and complaints procedures

3. **Resource Requirements**
   - Evaluate personnel competence requirements
   - Assess auditor qualification and training
   - Review resource allocation adequacy
   - Validate subcontractor management

4. **Information Requirements**
   - Assess public information accessibility
   - Review certification scheme documentation
   - Validate confidentiality procedures

   - Review certificate issuance procedures
   - Validate surveillance and recertification
   - Confirm suspension and withdrawal procedures

## ISO 17021 Certification Body Assessment

**Certification Processes:**
- **Application Review**: Client eligibility and scope determination
- **Audit Planning**: Audit team selection and planning
- **Stage 1 Audit**: Documentation review and readiness assessment
- **Stage 2 Audit**: On-site assessment and findings evaluation
- **Certification Decision**: Review and approval of certification
- **Surveillance**: Ongoing monitoring and reassessment
- **Recertification**: Full reassessment before certificate expiry

**Quality Assurance Mechanisms:**
- **Internal Audits**: Regular assessment of certification processes
- **Management Reviews**: Periodic evaluation of system effectiveness
- **External Assessments**: Accreditation body evaluations
- **Peer Reviews**: Inter-certification body quality assessments
