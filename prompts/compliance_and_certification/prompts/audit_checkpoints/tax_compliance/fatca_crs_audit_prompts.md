# FATCA and CRS Tax Compliance Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act) and CRS (Common Reporting Standard) tax compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### FATCA (Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act)
**Effective Date**: March 18, 2010 (enacted), phased implementation through 2017  
**Scope**: Foreign financial institutions and U.S. persons with foreign accounts  
**Key Requirements**: Identification and reporting of U.S. accounts, withholding on certain payments  
**Structure**: Intergovernmental agreements between U.S. and foreign jurisdictions  
**Benefits**: Improved tax compliance, reduced tax evasion, enhanced transparency

### CRS (Common Reporting Standard)
**Effective Date**: 2014 (developed by OECD), implementation from 2016  
**Scope**: Financial institutions in participating jurisdictions  
**Key Requirements**: Automatic exchange of financial account information  
**Structure**: Global standard for automatic exchange of information  
**Benefits**: Enhanced tax transparency, reduced cross-border tax evasion, improved compliance

## Regulatory Context
- **FATCA**: U.S. law targeting offshore tax evasion
- **CRS**: OECD standard for global tax information exchange
- **Implementation**: Through bilateral agreements and domestic legislation
- **Penalties**: Significant financial penalties for non-compliance
