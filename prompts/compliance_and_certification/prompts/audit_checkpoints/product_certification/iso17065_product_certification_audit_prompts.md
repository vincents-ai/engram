# ISO 17065 Product Certification Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 17065:2012 Conformity Assessment implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 17065:2012 Conformity Assessment - Requirements for Bodies Certifying Products, Processes and Services
**Effective Date**: September 15, 2012 (current version)  
**Scope**: Product certification bodies providing certification services  
**Key Principles**: Impartiality, competence, responsibility, open-mindedness, consistency, effectiveness, risk-based approach  
**Structure**: Requirements covering general requirements, structural requirements, resource requirements, process requirements, management system requirements  
**Benefits**: Consistent certification practices, enhanced product credibility, improved market confidence, regulatory compliance

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 17065**: International standard for product certification bodies
- **Accreditation Requirement**: Certification bodies must be accredited to ISO 17065
- **Product Focus**: Applicable to products, processes, and services
- **Market Access**: Required for many regulated product categories

## ISO 17065 Product Certification Assessment

**Certification Types:**
- **Type Testing**: Initial product testing and evaluation
- **Batch Testing**: Testing of product batches or lots
- **Audit Testing**: Periodic verification of certified products
- **Surveillance**: Ongoing monitoring of certified products

**Conformity Assessment Methods:**
- **Testing**: Laboratory testing and analysis
- **Inspection**: Physical inspection and verification
- **Assessment**: Technical evaluation and review
- **Certification**: Formal certification and marking
