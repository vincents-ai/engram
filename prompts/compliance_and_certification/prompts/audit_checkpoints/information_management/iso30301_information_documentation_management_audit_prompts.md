## Context and Background

### ISO 30301:2019 Information and Documentation - Management Systems for Records - Requirements
**Effective Date**: November 15, 2019 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations managing records and information throughout their lifecycle  
**Key Principles**: Accountability, integrity, protection, compliance, availability, retention, disposition  
**Structure**: Requirements covering records management policy, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, improvement  
**Benefits**: Improved information governance, regulatory compliance, better decision making, risk reduction

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 30301**: International standard for records management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **Lifecycle Approach**: Covers creation, capture, organization, storage, access, and disposal
- **Compliance Focus**: Supports legal, regulatory, and operational requirements

## ISO 30301 Records Management Assessment

**Records Lifecycle Phases:**
- **Creation/Capture**: Document creation, receipt, and capture
- **Organization/Storage**: Classification, indexing, and storage
- **Access/Use**: Retrieval, distribution, and utilization
- **Retention/Disposition**: Storage duration and final disposition

**Records Types:**
- **Vital Records**: Essential for business continuity
- **Important Records**: Required for legal/regulatory compliance
- **Useful Records**: Operational and historical value
- **Non-Essential Records**: Limited or no retention value
