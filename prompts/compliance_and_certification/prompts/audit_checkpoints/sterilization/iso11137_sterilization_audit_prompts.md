# ISO 11137 Sterilization Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 11137:2013 Sterilization of Health Care Products implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 11137:2013 Sterilization of Health Care Products - Radiation - Requirements for Development, Validation and Routine Control of a Sterilization Process for Medical Devices
**Effective Date**: April 15, 2013 (current version)  
**Scope**: Radiation sterilization of medical devices and healthcare products  
**Key Principles**: Sterility assurance, validation, routine control, documentation  
**Structure**: Requirements covering sterilization process development, validation, and control  
**Benefits**: Patient safety, product sterility assurance, regulatory compliance, quality assurance

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 11137**: International standard for radiation sterilization
- **Regulatory Requirement**: Required for radiation-sterilized medical devices
- **Sterility Assurance**: Ensures product safety and efficacy
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for sterilization validation
