# ISO 9241 and ISO 10075 Ergonomics Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 9241 series Ergonomics of Human-System Interaction and ISO 10075 series Mental Workload compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 9241 Series: Ergonomics of Human-System Interaction
**Effective Date**: Various dates for different parts (1990s-2020s)  
**Scope**: Software, hardware, and workplace design for human use  
**Key Principles**: Usability, accessibility, health and safety, productivity  
**Structure**: Comprehensive series covering human-system interaction aspects  
**Benefits**: Improved user performance, reduced errors, enhanced safety, better user satisfaction

### ISO 10075 Series: Ergonomic Principles Related to Mental Workload
**Effective Date**: Various dates for different parts (1990s-2000s)  
**Scope**: Mental workload assessment and management in work systems  
**Key Principles**: Workload assessment, fatigue prevention, stress management  
**Structure**: Standards for mental workload evaluation and control  
**Benefits**: Reduced mental fatigue, improved performance, better work-life balance, enhanced safety

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 9241**: International standards for human-system interaction
- **ISO 10075**: Standards for mental workload management
- **Ergonomics Focus**: Human factors in system design and work organization
- **Health and Safety**: Prevention of work-related musculoskeletal and mental health issues

   - Review self-descriptiveness requirements

   - Evaluate information clarity and discernibility
   - Assess conciseness and consistency

   - Review character and symbol design

   - Confirm viewing distance considerations

   - Assess user involvement procedures
   - Review design iteration methods
   - Validate usability testing procedures
