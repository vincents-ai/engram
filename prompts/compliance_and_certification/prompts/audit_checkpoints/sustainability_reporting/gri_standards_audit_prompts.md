## Context and Background

### GRI Standards (Global Reporting Initiative)
**Effective Date**: 2016 (current Universal Standards), updated regularly  
**Scope**: Organizations reporting on economic, environmental, and social impacts  
**Key Principles**: Stakeholder inclusiveness, sustainability context, materiality, completeness  
**Structure**: Universal Standards + Topic-specific Standards covering 100+ disclosure topics  
**Benefits**: Transparent reporting, stakeholder engagement, improved decision-making, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **GRI Standards**: Global framework for sustainability reporting
- **Universal Application**: Applicable to organizations of all sizes and sectors
- **Integration**: Foundation for many national and regional reporting requirements
- **Evolution**: Regularly updated based on stakeholder feedback and emerging issues
