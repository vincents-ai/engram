This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 55001:2014 Asset Management System implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 55001:2014 Asset Management - Management Systems - Requirements
**Effective Date**: January 15, 2014  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to establish, implement, maintain, and improve asset management systems  
**Key Principles**: Value creation, alignment with organizational objectives, leadership commitment, risk-based decision making  
**Structure**: 10 clauses covering Context, Leadership, Planning, Support, Operation, Performance Evaluation, Improvement  
**Benefits**: Optimized asset performance, reduced costs, improved risk management, enhanced decision making

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 55001**: International standard for asset management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **Asset Types**: Physical, financial, intangible, and human assets
- **Lifecycle Approach**: Considers asset management throughout the asset lifecycle

**ISO 55001 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Context of the Organization (Clause 4)**
   - Evaluate internal/external issue identification
   - Assess interested party needs and expectations
   - Review asset management system scope
   - Validate AMS planning and objectives

2. **Leadership (Clause 5)**
   - Assess leadership and commitment to AMS
   - Review asset management policy establishment
   - Validate organizational roles and responsibilities
   - Confirm planning for AMS changes

3. **Planning (Clause 6)**
   - Evaluate asset management objectives
   - Assess risk and opportunity management
   - Review planning for AMS changes
   - Validate planning of changes

4. **Support (Clause 7)**
   - Assess resource provision for AMS
   - Review competence and awareness requirements
   - Validate communication processes
   - Confirm documented information management

5. **Operation (Clause 8)**
   - Evaluate asset management strategy development
   - Assess asset management plans implementation
   - Review asset information management
   - Validate risk management procedures

6. **Performance Evaluation (Clause 9)**
   - Assess monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation
   - Review internal audit processes
   - Validate management review processes
   - Confirm performance measurement accuracy

7. **Improvement (Clause 10)**
   - Evaluate improvement opportunities
   - Assess nonconformity and corrective action
   - Review continual improvement processes
   - Validate preventive action mechanisms
