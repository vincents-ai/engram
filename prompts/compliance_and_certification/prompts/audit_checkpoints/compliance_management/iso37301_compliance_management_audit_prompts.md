## Context and Background

### ISO 37301:2021 Compliance Management Systems - Requirements with Guidance for Use
**Effective Date**: April 15, 2021 (current version)  
**Scope**: Compliance management systems for organizations of all types and sizes  
**Key Principles**: Leadership, risk-based approach, stakeholder engagement, continual improvement, transparency, accountability  
**Structure**: Requirements covering compliance policy, objectives, planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, improvement  
**Benefits**: Enhanced compliance effectiveness, reduced compliance risks, improved organizational culture, better stakeholder trust

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 37301**: International standard for compliance management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **Risk-Based**: Emphasizes risk assessment and mitigation
- **Global Application**: Applicable to all compliance requirements

## ISO 37301 Compliance Risk Assessment

**Compliance Risk Categories:**
- **Legal Compliance**: Laws, regulations, court decisions
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Industry regulations, licensing requirements
- **Ethical Compliance**: Codes of conduct, ethical standards
- **Contractual Compliance**: Contract terms, service level agreements
- **Industry Compliance**: Industry standards, best practices

**Compliance Control Framework:**
- **Preventive Controls**: Policies, procedures, training
- **Detective Controls**: Monitoring, auditing, reporting
- **Corrective Controls**: Investigation, remediation, improvement
- **Directive Controls**: Leadership, governance, oversight
