# ISO 19600 Compliance Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 19600:2014 Compliance Management System implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 19600:2014 Compliance Management Systems - Guidelines
**Effective Date**: December 15, 2014  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to establish, develop, implement, evaluate, maintain, and improve compliance management systems  
**Key Principles**: Leadership, risk-based approach, stakeholder engagement, continual improvement  
**Structure**: Guidelines covering governance, risk management, controls, culture, and performance  
**Benefits**: Enhanced compliance effectiveness, reduced compliance risks, improved organizational culture, better stakeholder trust

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 19600**: International guideline for compliance management systems
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides guidance rather than requirements for certification
- **Integration**: Applicable to any compliance framework or regulatory requirement
- **Global Application**: Suitable for organizations of all sizes and sectors

**ISO 19600 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Governance and Leadership**
   - Evaluate compliance governance structure
   - Assess leadership commitment to compliance
   - Review compliance policy and objectives
   - Validate organizational roles and responsibilities

2. **Compliance Risk Assessment**
   - Assess compliance risk identification processes
   - Review risk analysis and evaluation procedures
   - Validate risk prioritization mechanisms
   - Confirm risk mitigation strategies

3. **Compliance Controls and Measures**
   - Evaluate preventive control implementation
   - Assess detective control effectiveness
   - Review corrective action procedures
   - Validate control monitoring mechanisms

4. **Compliance Culture and Communication**
   - Assess organizational compliance culture
   - Review communication and training programs
   - Validate stakeholder engagement processes
   - Confirm reporting and whistleblowing mechanisms

5. **Compliance Performance Monitoring**
   - Evaluate compliance monitoring procedures
   - Assess key performance indicator development
   - Review compliance reporting processes
   - Validate performance measurement accuracy

6. **Compliance Assurance and Auditing**
   - Assess internal compliance audit processes
   - Review external assurance mechanisms
   - Validate audit finding remediation
   - Confirm continuous improvement processes

7. **Compliance Incident Management**
   - Evaluate incident detection procedures
   - Assess incident investigation processes
   - Review incident response mechanisms
   - Validate lesson learned implementation
