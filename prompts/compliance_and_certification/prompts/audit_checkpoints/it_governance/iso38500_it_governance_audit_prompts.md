# ISO 38500 IT Governance Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 38500:2015 IT Governance implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 38500:2015 Information Technology - Governance of IT for the Organization
**Effective Date**: June 15, 2015 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking effective governance of IT investments and resources  
**Key Principles**: Responsibility, strategy, acquisition, performance, conformance, human behavior  
**Structure**: Guidance covering IT governance principles, model, and evaluation  
**Benefits**: Better IT investment decisions, improved IT performance, enhanced stakeholder confidence, strategic alignment

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 38500**: International standard for IT governance
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides guidance for governing bodies
- **Board Level**: Focuses on executive and board responsibilities
- **Principles-Based**: Six core principles for IT governance

**ISO 38500 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Principle 1: Responsibility**
   - Evaluate governance body IT responsibility assignment
   - Assess IT decision-making authority definition
   - Review accountability mechanisms
   - Validate responsibility communication

2. **Principle 2: Strategy**
   - Assess IT strategy alignment with business objectives
   - Review IT strategic planning processes
   - Validate IT investment prioritization
   - Confirm strategy communication and implementation

3. **Principle 3: Acquisition**
   - Evaluate IT acquisition decision processes
   - Assess supplier selection criteria
   - Review contract management procedures
   - Validate acquisition risk management

4. **Principle 4: Performance**
   - Assess IT performance measurement systems
   - Review performance monitoring procedures
   - Validate performance reporting mechanisms
   - Confirm performance improvement processes

5. **Principle 5: Conformance**
   - Evaluate IT compliance with legal requirements
   - Assess regulatory compliance monitoring
   - Review internal control effectiveness
   - Validate compliance reporting procedures

6. **Principle 6: Human Behavior**
   - Assess IT governance culture development
   - Review stakeholder IT awareness
   - Validate IT decision-making ethics
   - Confirm human factor consideration in IT governance

7. **Governance Model Application**
   - Evaluate governance model selection
   - Assess governance structure effectiveness
   - Review governance process implementation
   - Validate governance model adaptation

8. **Governance Evaluation**
   - Assess governance effectiveness measurement
   - Review governance performance evaluation
   - Validate governance improvement processes
   - Confirm governance benchmarking
