## Context and Background

### ISO 17020:2012 Conformity Assessment - Requirements for the Operation of Various Types of Bodies Performing Inspection
**Effective Date**: March 1, 2012 (current version)  
**Scope**: Inspection bodies performing inspections and issuing inspection reports/certificates  
**Key Principles**: Impartiality, competence, responsibility, open-mindedness, consistency, effectiveness, risk-based approach  
**Structure**: Requirements covering general requirements, structural requirements, resource requirements, process requirements, management system requirements  
**Benefits**: Consistent inspection practices, enhanced inspection credibility, improved stakeholder confidence, regulatory compliance

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 17020**: International standard for inspection bodies
- **Accreditation Requirement**: Inspection bodies must be accredited to ISO 17020
- **Inspection Types**: Type A, B, C based on relationship to inspected organization
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for inspection competence

## ISO 17020 Inspection Body Assessment

**Inspection Body Types:**
- **Type A**: Independent third-party inspection bodies
- **Type B**: Inspection bodies working on behalf of organizations
- **Type C**: Inspection bodies that are part of the organization being inspected

**Inspection Categories:**
- **Product Inspection**: Examination of products for conformity
- **Process Inspection**: Evaluation of processes and methods
- **Installation Inspection**: Assessment of installations and systems
- **Service Inspection**: Review of services and deliverables
