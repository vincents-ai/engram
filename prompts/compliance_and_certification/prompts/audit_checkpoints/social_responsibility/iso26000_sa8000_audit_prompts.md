# ISO 26000 and SA 8000 Social Responsibility Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 26000:2010 Social Responsibility and SA 8000:2014 Social Accountability compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 26000:2010 Guidance on Social Responsibility
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2010  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to operate in socially responsible manner  
**Key Principles**: Accountability, transparency, ethical behavior, respect for stakeholder interests, respect for rule of law, respect for international norms  
**Structure**: Guidance document covering core subjects and implementation  
**Benefits**: Enhanced reputation, improved stakeholder relationships, better risk management, sustainable development

### SA 8000:2014 Social Accountability Standard
**Effective Date**: October 29, 2014 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking certification for social accountability  
**Key Requirements**: Child labor, forced labor, health and safety, freedom of association, discrimination, disciplinary practices, working hours, compensation  
**Structure**: Standard with requirements for certification  
**Benefits**: Improved working conditions, ethical supply chains, certification credibility, market access

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 26000**: International guidance for social responsibility
- **SA 8000**: Certifiable standard for social accountability
- **Voluntary Standards**: Not legally binding but increasingly required by customers
- **Global Application**: Applicable to organizations of all sizes and sectors
