# ISO 22716 Cosmetics GMP Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 22716:2007 Cosmetics - Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 22716:2007 Cosmetics - Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) - Guidelines on Good Manufacturing Practices
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2007 (current version)  
**Scope**: Cosmetics manufacturing organizations seeking GMP compliance  
**Key Principles**: Quality assurance, personnel hygiene, premises and equipment, documentation, production, quality control, contract manufacturing, complaints and recalls  
**Structure**: Guidelines covering all aspects of cosmetics manufacturing  
**Benefits**: Product safety assurance, regulatory compliance, quality consistency, consumer protection

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 22716**: International standard for cosmetics Good Manufacturing Practices
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Aligns with EU Cosmetics Regulation and other global requirements
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensures consistent product quality and safety
- **Consumer Protection**: Prevents contamination and adverse reactions

**ISO 22716 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Personnel**
   - Evaluate personnel qualification requirements
   - Assess hygiene and health procedures
   - Review training program effectiveness
   - Validate personnel record maintenance

2. **Premises**
   - Assess facility design and layout
   - Review cleaning and sanitation procedures
   - Validate environmental control measures
   - Confirm waste management procedures

3. **Equipment**
   - Evaluate equipment qualification procedures
   - Assess maintenance and calibration schedules
   - Review equipment cleaning validation
   - Validate equipment usage documentation

4. **Raw Materials and Packaging**
   - Assess supplier qualification procedures
   - Review incoming material testing
   - Validate material storage conditions
   - Confirm material traceability requirements

5. **Production**
   - Evaluate batch manufacturing procedures
   - Assess production record completeness
   - Review in-process control measures
   - Validate production deviation handling

6. **Finished Products**
   - Assess finished product testing requirements
   - Review product release procedures
   - Validate product stability testing
   - Confirm product labeling compliance

7. **Quality Control Laboratory**
   - Evaluate laboratory facility adequacy
   - Assess testing method validation
   - Review equipment calibration procedures
   - Validate test result documentation

8. **Treatment of Non-Conforming Products**
   - Assess non-conformance identification procedures
   - Review investigation and root cause analysis
   - Validate corrective action implementation
   - Confirm product disposition procedures

9. **Waste Management**
   - Evaluate waste classification procedures
   - Assess waste handling and storage
   - Review waste treatment methods
   - Validate waste disposal documentation

10. **Subcontracting**
    - Assess subcontractor qualification procedures
    - Review subcontracting agreement requirements
    - Validate subcontractor oversight mechanisms
    - Confirm subcontracting record maintenance

11. **Complaints and Recalls**
    - Evaluate complaint handling procedures
    - Assess complaint investigation processes
    - Review recall procedure effectiveness
    - Validate complaint record maintenance

12. **Documentation and Records**
    - Assess documentation control procedures
    - Review record retention requirements
    - Validate document change control
    - Confirm record traceability requirements
