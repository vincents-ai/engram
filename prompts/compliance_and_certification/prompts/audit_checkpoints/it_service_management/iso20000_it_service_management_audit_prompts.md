# ISO 20000 IT Service Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 20000-1:2018 IT Service Management implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 20000-1:2018 Information Technology - Service Management - Part 1: Service Management System Requirements
**Effective Date**: September 15, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: IT service management systems for service providers  
**Key Principles**: Service management system, design and transition, delivery and relationship, resolution and fulfillment, relationship and supply  
**Structure**: Requirements covering service management planning, design, transition, delivery, relationship management  
**Benefits**: Improved service quality, better customer satisfaction, enhanced operational efficiency, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 20000**: International standard for IT service management
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **ITIL Alignment**: Compatible with ITIL framework
- **Service Focus**: Emphasizes customer requirements and service quality

**ISO 20000 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Understanding the Organization and Its Context**
   - Evaluate internal/external issue identification
   - Assess interested party needs and expectations
   - Review service management system scope
   - Validate SMS planning and objectives

2. **Leadership**
   - Assess leadership commitment to service management
   - Review service management policy establishment
   - Validate organizational roles and responsibilities
   - Confirm planning for SMS changes

3. **Planning**
   - Evaluate service management objectives setting
   - Assess risk and opportunity management
   - Review planning for SMS changes
   - Validate planning of changes

4. **Support**
   - Assess resource provision for SMS
   - Review competence and awareness training
   - Validate communication processes
   - Confirm documented information management

5. **Operation - Service Management Planning and Design**
   - Evaluate service portfolio design
   - Assess service level management
   - Review supplier management
   - Validate service reporting

6. **Operation - Service Management Transition**
   - Assess change management processes
   - Review service transition planning
   - Validate release and deployment
   - Confirm service validation and testing

7. **Operation - Service Management Delivery**
   - Evaluate service delivery processes
   - Assess incident management
   - Review problem management
   - Validate service request fulfillment

8. **Operation - Service Management Relationship**
   - Assess customer relationship management
   - Review service level agreements
   - Validate customer satisfaction monitoring
   - Confirm relationship improvement processes

9. **Performance Evaluation**
   - Assess monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation
   - Review internal audit processes
   - Validate management review processes
   - Confirm performance measurement accuracy

10. **Improvement**
    - Evaluate improvement opportunity identification
    - Assess nonconformity and corrective action

## ISO 20000 Service Management Assessment

**Service Management Processes:**
- **Service Portfolio Management**: Service catalog, service pipeline, retired services
- **Service Level Management**: SLA definition, monitoring, reporting
- **Supplier Management**: Supplier agreements, performance monitoring
- **Change Management**: Change requests, impact assessment, implementation
- **Release and Deployment**: Release planning, testing, deployment
- **Incident Management**: Incident logging, diagnosis, resolution
- **Problem Management**: Problem identification, root cause analysis, resolution
- **Service Request Fulfillment**: Request logging, approval, fulfillment
- **Customer Relationship Management**: Customer communication, satisfaction monitoring

**Service Quality Metrics:**
- **Availability**: Service uptime percentage
- **Reliability**: Mean time between failures
- **Performance**: Response time, throughput
- **Security**: Security incident rates
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Survey results, feedback analysis
