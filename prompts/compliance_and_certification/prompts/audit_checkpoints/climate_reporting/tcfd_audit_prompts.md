## Context and Background

### TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures)
**Effective Date**: 2017 (recommendations published), voluntary adoption  
**Scope**: Organizations disclosing climate-related financial risks and opportunities  
**Key Elements**: Governance, Strategy, Risk Management, Metrics and Targets  
**Structure**: 11 recommended disclosures covering current and future impacts  
**Benefits**: Enhanced investor confidence, improved risk management, regulatory compliance preparation

## Regulatory Context
- **TCFD**: Voluntary framework for climate-related financial disclosures
- **Increasing Adoption**: Required or recommended by regulators in multiple jurisdictions
- **Integration**: Foundation for climate-related reporting in various frameworks
- **Evolution**: Recommendations being updated based on implementation experience
