# ISO 9001 Quality Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management System implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems
**Effective Date**: September 15, 2015 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations of all sizes and sectors seeking to improve quality management  
**Key Principles**: Customer focus, Leadership, Engagement of people, Process approach, Improvement, Evidence-based decision making, Relationship management  
**Structure**: 10 clauses covering Context, Leadership, Planning, Support, Operation, Performance Evaluation, Improvement  
**Benefits**: Improved customer satisfaction, enhanced operational efficiency, better risk management, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 9001**: International standard for quality management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **Integration**: Compatible with other management system standards (ISO 14001, ISO 27001)
- **Continuous Improvement**: PDCA cycle (Plan-Do-Check-Act) embedded throughout

## ISO 9001 Process Audit Prompts

### Quality Management Process Assessment

**Process Categories:**
- **Management Processes**: [PLANNING_CONTROL_IMPROVEMENT]
- **Core Processes**: [PRODUCT_REALIZATION_SUPPORT]
- **Support Processes**: [RESOURCE_MANAGEMENT_INFRASTRUCTURE]

**Process Audit Checklist:**

1. **Process Definition and Documentation**
   - Verify process purpose and scope
   - Assess process input/output identification
   - Review process owner assignment
   - Validate process performance measures

2. **Process Controls and Monitoring**
   - Evaluate process control mechanisms
   - Assess monitoring and measurement
   - Review process performance analysis
   - Validate corrective action procedures

3. **Process Improvement**
   - Assess improvement opportunity identification
   - Review improvement planning and implementation
   - Validate improvement effectiveness measurement
   - Confirm lesson learned incorporation

**Process Maturity Assessment:**
- **Level 1 (Initial)**: Processes are ad hoc and chaotic
- **Level 2 (Repeatable)**: Processes are documented and repeatable
- **Level 3 (Defined)**: Processes are standardized and integrated
- **Level 4 (Managed)**: Processes are measured and controlled
- **Level 5 (Optimizing)**: Processes are continuously improved
