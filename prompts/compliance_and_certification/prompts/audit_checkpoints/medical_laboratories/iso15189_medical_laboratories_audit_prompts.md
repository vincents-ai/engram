# ISO 15189 Medical Laboratories Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 15189:2012 Medical Laboratories implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 15189:2012 Medical Laboratories - Requirements for Quality and Competence
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2012 (current version)  
**Scope**: Medical laboratories seeking quality and competence in testing and calibration  
**Key Principles**: Patient safety, quality management, competence, continuous improvement  
**Structure**: Requirements covering general requirements, structural requirements, resource requirements, process requirements, management system requirements  
**Benefits**: Enhanced patient safety, improved test quality, regulatory compliance, professional competence

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 15189**: International standard for medical laboratory quality and competence
- **Accreditation Requirement**: Medical laboratories must be accredited to ISO 15189
- **Patient Safety**: Focus on accurate and reliable test results
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for medical laboratory competence

## ISO 15189 Medical Laboratory Assessment

**Laboratory Disciplines:**
- **Clinical Chemistry**: Blood analysis, hormone testing, drug monitoring
- **Hematology**: Blood cell counting, coagulation studies
- **Microbiology**: Infectious disease testing, culture analysis
- **Immunology**: Antibody testing, autoimmune disease diagnosis
- **Molecular Diagnostics**: Genetic testing, PCR analysis

**Quality Assurance Processes:**
- **Internal Quality Control**: Daily quality checks and controls
- **External Quality Assessment**: Participation in proficiency testing
- **Method Validation**: Verification of test accuracy and precision
- **Equipment Maintenance**: Regular calibration and maintenance
