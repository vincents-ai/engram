## Context and Background

### ISO 20400:2017 Sustainable Procurement - Guidance
**Effective Date**: January 15, 2017 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to integrate sustainability into procurement processes  
**Key Principles**: Integration, strategy, engagement, capacity building, transparency, risk management  
**Structure**: Guidance covering sustainable procurement planning, implementation, and evaluation  
**Benefits**: Reduced environmental impact, improved social outcomes, enhanced supplier relationships, cost savings

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 20400**: International guidance for sustainable procurement
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides framework for sustainable purchasing
- **Supply Chain Focus**: Influences entire supplier ecosystem
- **Triple Bottom Line**: Economic, environmental, social considerations
