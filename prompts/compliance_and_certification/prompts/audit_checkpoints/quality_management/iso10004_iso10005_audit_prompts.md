This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 10004:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Monitoring and Measurement and ISO 10005:2018 Quality Management Systems - Guidelines for Quality Plans compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 10004:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Monitoring and Measurement
**Effective Date**: September 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to monitor and measure customer satisfaction  
**Key Principles**: Systematic approach, data-driven decisions, continuous improvement  
**Structure**: Guidelines for customer satisfaction monitoring and measurement systems  
**Benefits**: Better understanding of customer needs, improved decision making, enhanced customer relationships, competitive advantage

### ISO 10005:2018 Quality Management Systems - Guidelines for Quality Plans
**Effective Date**: September 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations developing and implementing quality plans  
**Key Principles**: Planning approach, stakeholder involvement, risk-based thinking  
**Structure**: Guidelines for quality planning processes and documentation  
**Benefits**: Improved project outcomes, better resource allocation, enhanced quality assurance, reduced risks

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 10004**: Guidelines for customer satisfaction measurement
- **ISO 10005**: Guidelines for quality planning and management
- **Quality Focus**: Systematic approaches to quality management
- **Continuous Improvement**: Data-driven decision making and planning
