This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 10006:2017 Quality Management Systems - Guidelines for Quality Management in Projects and ISO 10007:2017 Quality Management Systems - Guidelines for Configuration Management compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 10006:2017 Quality Management Systems - Guidelines for Quality Management in Projects
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2017 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations managing projects with quality management integration  
**Key Principles**: Project quality planning, stakeholder involvement, risk management, continuous improvement  
**Structure**: Guidelines for quality management application in project environments  
**Benefits**: Improved project outcomes, better stakeholder satisfaction, reduced project risks, enhanced quality assurance

### ISO 10007:2017 Quality Management Systems - Guidelines for Configuration Management
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2017 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations managing configuration of products and services  
**Key Principles**: Configuration identification, control, status accounting, verification  
**Structure**: Guidelines for configuration management processes and systems  
**Benefits**: Improved product consistency, better change control, enhanced traceability, reduced errors

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 10006**: Quality management guidelines for project environments
- **ISO 10007**: Configuration management guidelines for quality systems
- **Project Focus**: Quality integration throughout project lifecycle
- **Configuration Control**: Systematic management of product and service configurations
