# ISO 19011 and ISO 10001 Quality Management Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 19011:2018 Guidelines for Auditing Management Systems and ISO 10001:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 19011:2018 Guidelines for Auditing Management Systems
**Effective Date**: July 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations conducting internal or external audits of management systems  
**Key Principles**: Integrity, fair presentation, due professional care, independence, evidence-based approach, risk-based approach  
**Structure**: Guidelines covering audit program management, audit conduct, auditor competence  
**Benefits**: Consistent audit practices, improved audit quality, enhanced auditor competence, better management system performance

### ISO 10001:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Guidelines for Codes of Conduct for Organizations
**Effective Date**: September 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to enhance customer satisfaction through codes of conduct  
**Key Areas**: Customer-focused culture, complaint handling, customer communication, customer satisfaction measurement  
**Structure**: Guidelines for developing and implementing customer satisfaction codes  
**Benefits**: Improved customer loyalty, enhanced reputation, better customer relationships, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 19011**: International guidelines for management system auditing
- **ISO 10001**: Guidelines for customer satisfaction management
- **Voluntary Standards**: Provide guidance for best practices
- **Integration**: Applicable to all management system standards
