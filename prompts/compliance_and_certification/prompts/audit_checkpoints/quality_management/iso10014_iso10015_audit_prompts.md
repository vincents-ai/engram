This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 10014:2021 Quality Management Systems - Managing an Organization for Quality Results and ISO 10015:2019 Quality Management - Guidelines for Training compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 10014:2021 Quality Management Systems - Managing an Organization for Quality Results
**Effective Date**: April 1, 2021 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to achieve quality results through effective management  
**Key Principles**: Leadership, customer focus, process approach, improvement, evidence-based decision making, relationship management  
**Structure**: Guidelines for managing organizations to achieve quality results  
**Benefits**: Improved organizational performance, better quality outcomes, enhanced stakeholder satisfaction, competitive advantage

### ISO 10015:2019 Quality Management - Guidelines for Training
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2019 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations developing and implementing training programs  
**Key Principles**: Systematic approach, competency development, training effectiveness, continuous improvement  
**Structure**: Guidelines for training planning, implementation, and evaluation  
**Benefits**: Improved employee competence, enhanced performance, better quality outcomes, organizational development

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 10014**: Guidelines for achieving quality results through management
- **ISO 10015**: Guidelines for effective training and competence development
- **Organizational Development**: Focus on people, processes, and performance
- **Quality Excellence**: Systematic approaches to quality management
