# ISO 10002 and ISO 10003 Quality Management Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 10002:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Complaint Handling and ISO 10003:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Dispute Resolution compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 10002:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Complaint Handling
**Effective Date**: September 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to handle customer complaints effectively  
**Key Principles**: Customer-focused approach, complaint resolution, continuous improvement  
**Structure**: Guidelines for complaint handling systems and processes  
**Benefits**: Improved customer satisfaction, enhanced reputation, better customer relationships, reduced complaints

### ISO 10003:2018 Quality Management - Customer Satisfaction - Dispute Resolution
**Effective Date**: September 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to resolve customer disputes fairly and efficiently  
**Key Principles**: Impartial dispute resolution, customer satisfaction, process efficiency  
**Structure**: Guidelines for dispute resolution systems and procedures  
**Benefits**: Fair dispute resolution, improved customer trust, legal compliance, reduced litigation

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 10002**: Guidelines for effective complaint handling
- **ISO 10003**: Guidelines for impartial dispute resolution
- **Customer Focus**: Emphasis on customer satisfaction and relationship management
- **Process Orientation**: Systematic approaches to complaint and dispute management
