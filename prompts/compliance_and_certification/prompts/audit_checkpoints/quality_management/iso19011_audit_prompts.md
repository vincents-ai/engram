## Context and Background

### ISO 19011:2018 Guidelines for Auditing Management Systems
**Effective Date**: July 1, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations conducting internal or external audits of management systems  
**Key Principles**: Integrity, fair presentation, due professional care, independence, evidence-based approach, risk-based approach  
**Structure**: Guidelines covering audit program management, audit conduct, auditor competence  
**Benefits**: Consistent audit practices, improved audit quality, enhanced auditor competence, better management system performance

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 19011**: International guidelines for management system auditing
- **Universal Application**: Applicable to all management system standards
- **Audit Quality**: Establishes standards for audit processes and competence
- **Continuous Improvement**: Supports audit program enhancement
