# ISO 10018 and ISO 10019 Quality Management Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 10018:2020 Quality Management - Guidance for People Involvement and ISO 10019:2005 Guidance on the Selection and Use of Quality Management and Customer Satisfaction compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 10018:2020 Quality Management - Guidance for People Involvement
**Effective Date**: April 1, 2020 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to involve people in quality management  
**Key Principles**: Leadership, communication, competence, motivation, engagement  
**Structure**: Guidelines for people involvement in quality management systems  
**Benefits**: Improved employee engagement, better quality outcomes, enhanced organizational performance, stronger quality culture

### ISO 10019:2005 Guidance on the Selection and Use of Quality Management and Customer Satisfaction
**Effective Date**: 2005  
**Scope**: Organizations selecting and using quality management and customer satisfaction methods  
**Key Principles**: Method selection, implementation, evaluation, improvement  
**Structure**: Guidance on selecting appropriate quality and customer satisfaction methods  
**Benefits**: Better method selection, improved implementation effectiveness, enhanced customer satisfaction, optimized resource use

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 10018**: Guidance for engaging people in quality management
- **ISO 10019**: Guidance for selecting quality management methods
- **People-Centric**: Focus on human factors in quality management
- **Method Selection**: Systematic approach to quality tool selection
