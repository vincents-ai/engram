This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health and Safety Management System implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health and Safety Management System
**Effective Date**: March 12, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations seeking to improve occupational health and safety performance  
**Key Principles**: Leadership, worker participation, risk-based approach, continual improvement  
**Structure**: 10 clauses covering Context, Leadership, Planning, Support, Operation, Performance Evaluation, Improvement  
**Benefits**: Reduced workplace accidents, improved employee health, regulatory compliance, enhanced reputation

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 45001**: International standard for occupational health and safety management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **Integration**: Compatible with other management system standards (ISO 9001, ISO 14001)
- **Worker Participation**: Emphasizes consultation and participation of workers

## ISO 45001 Hazard and Risk Assessment

- **Biological Hazards**: Bacteria, viruses, fungi, parasites
- **Ergonomic Hazards**: Repetitive strain, poor posture, heavy lifting
- **Psychosocial Hazards**: Stress, violence, bullying, fatigue
