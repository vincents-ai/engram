# ISO 21500 Project Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 21500:2021 Project Management implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 21500:2021 Project Management - Guidance
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2021 (current version)  
**Scope**: Project management processes and practices  
**Key Principles**: Integrated approach, stakeholder involvement, value creation, systems thinking, leadership, tailoring, learning, agility, resilience  
**Structure**: Guidance covering project management processes and competencies  
**Benefits**: Improved project success rates, better stakeholder satisfaction, enhanced organizational capability, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 21500**: International standard for project management
- **Voluntary Standard**: Provides guidance rather than requirements
- **Global Application**: Applicable to projects of all sizes and types
- **Integration**: Compatible with other management standards

## ISO 21500 Project Management Processes

**Core Project Management Processes:**
- **Project Initiation**: Project charter, stakeholder identification, initial planning
- **Project Planning**: Scope, schedule, cost, quality, risk, communication planning
- **Project Execution**: Team development, quality assurance, information distribution
- **Project Monitoring and Control**: Performance measurement, change control, risk monitoring
- **Project Closing**: Final deliverables, lessons learned, project documentation

**Supporting Processes:**
- **Integration Management**: Project plan development, execution, monitoring, change control
- **Scope Management**: Requirements collection, scope definition, verification, control
- **Time Management**: Activity definition, sequencing, duration estimating, schedule control
- **Cost Management**: Cost estimating, budgeting, cost control
- **Quality Management**: Quality planning, assurance, control
- **Human Resource Management**: Team planning, acquisition, development, management
- **Communications Management**: Communications planning, information distribution, performance reporting
- **Risk Management**: Risk identification, qualitative/quantitative analysis, response planning
- **Procurement Management**: Procurement planning, contract administration, contract closure
