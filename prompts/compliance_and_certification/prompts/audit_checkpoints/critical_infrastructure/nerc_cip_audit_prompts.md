# NERC CIP (Critical Infrastructure Protection) Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for NERC CIP compliance assessment in the electric sector.

## Context and Background

### NERC CIP (Critical Infrastructure Protection)
**Effective Date**: 2008 (Version 5 currently in effect, Version 6 in development)  
**Scope**: Bulk electric system owners, operators, and users in North America  
**Key Standards**: CIP-002 through CIP-014 covering security management controls  
**Penalties**: Civil penalties up to $1 million per day per violation  
**Enforcement**: NERC and Regional Entity oversight, FERC approval

## Regulatory Context
- **NERC CIP**: Mandatory reliability standards for North American electric grid
- **Bulk Electric System**: Generation, transmission, and distribution facilities
- **Regional Entities**: Eight regional organizations oversee compliance
- **CIP Versions**: Evolve based on threat landscape and technology changes
