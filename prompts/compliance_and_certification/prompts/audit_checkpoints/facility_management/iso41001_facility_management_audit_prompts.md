# ISO 41001 Facility Management System Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 41001:2018 Facility Management implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 41001:2018 Facility Management - Management Systems for the Delivery of Facility Management Services
**Effective Date**: April 15, 2018 (current version)  
**Scope**: Organizations providing facility management services  
**Key Principles**: Customer focus, leadership, engagement of people, process approach, improvement, evidence-based decision making, relationship management  
**Structure**: Requirements covering facility management planning, support, operation, performance evaluation, improvement  
**Benefits**: Improved facility service quality, better stakeholder satisfaction, enhanced operational efficiency, competitive advantage

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 41001**: International standard for facility management systems
- **Certification**: Third-party certification available through accredited bodies
- **Service Focus**: Emphasizes customer requirements and service quality
- **Lifecycle Approach**: Covers planning, delivery, and improvement of facility services

**ISO 41001 Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Context of the Organization**
   - Evaluate facility management context analysis
   - Assess interested party service expectations
   - Review facility management system scope
   - Validate FM objectives setting

2. **Leadership**
   - Assess leadership commitment to facility management
   - Review facility management policy establishment
   - Validate organizational roles and responsibilities
   - Confirm planning for FM system changes

3. **Planning**
   - Evaluate facility service planning
   - Assess facility management objective setting
   - Review planning for FM system changes
   - Validate planning of changes

4. **Support**
   - Assess resource provision for FM
   - Review competence and awareness training
   - Validate communication processes
   - Confirm documented information management

5. **Operation - Service Delivery**
   - Evaluate service delivery planning
   - Assess service level agreement management
   - Review service performance monitoring
   - Validate customer satisfaction measurement

6. **Operation - Service Management**
   - Assess facility service management processes
   - Review maintenance and operations management
   - Validate risk management procedures
   - Confirm emergency response planning

7. **Operation - Relationship Management**
   - Evaluate customer relationship management
   - Assess stakeholder communication processes
   - Review feedback and complaint handling
   - Validate relationship improvement mechanisms

8. **Performance Evaluation**
   - Assess monitoring, measurement, analysis, and evaluation
   - Review internal audit processes
   - Validate management review processes
   - Confirm performance measurement accuracy

9. **Improvement**
   - Evaluate improvement opportunity identification
   - Assess nonconformity and corrective action
   - Review continual improvement processes
   - Validate preventive action mechanisms

## ISO 41001 Facility Service Assessment

**Facility Service Categories:**

**Facility Performance Metrics:**
- **Service Quality**: Response time, resolution time, customer satisfaction
- **Operational Efficiency**: Cost per square foot, energy consumption, waste reduction
- **Asset Management**: Equipment reliability, preventive maintenance effectiveness
