# Auditor Qualifications Master Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for verifying auditor qualifications and certifications across all major compliance domains.

## Context and Background

### Auditor Qualification Requirements
**Scope**: Verification of auditor certifications, experience, and competence for conducting compliance audits  
**Standards**: ISACA, IIA, (ISC)², IRCA, State Boards, and other certification bodies  
**Purpose**: Ensure auditors have required qualifications for specific compliance standards  
**Relevance**: Critical for audit quality, regulatory compliance, and stakeholder confidence

## Regulatory Context
- **Certification Bodies**: ISACA, IIA, (ISC)², IRCA, State Boards
- **Professional Standards**: Codes of ethics, CPE requirements, experience verification
- **Audit Quality**: Qualified auditors ensure reliable and credible audit results
- **Regulatory Requirements**: Many standards require certified auditors
