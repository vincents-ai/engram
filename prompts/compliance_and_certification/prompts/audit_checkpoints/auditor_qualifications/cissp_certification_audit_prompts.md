## Context and Background

### CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional)
**Issuing Body**: (ISC)² (International Information System Security Certification Consortium)  
**Effective Date**: 1993 (ongoing certification program)  
**Scope**: Information security, risk management, security architecture, operations  
**Requirements**: 5 years experience, passing exam, endorsement, adherence to Code of Ethics  
**Renewal**: Every 3 years with 120 CPE credits  
**Relevance**: Required for FedRAMP, CMMC, NERC CIP, advanced ISO 27001 audits

## Regulatory Context
- **(ISC)² Standards**: Global standards for information security professionals
- **CPE Requirements**: Continuing professional education mandatory
- **Code of Ethics**: Professional conduct and confidentiality requirements
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for security expertise

**CISSP Compliance Assessment:**

1. **Certification Status Verification**
   - Evaluate CISSP certification validity and expiration date
   - Assess certification number and registration confirmation
   - Review certification maintenance requirements
   - Validate certification renewal status

2. **Experience Requirements Assessment**
   - Assess 5 years of paid, full-time security experience
   - Review experience documentation and verification
   - Validate experience relevance to security domains
   - Confirm experience quality and progression

3. **Examination Requirements Verification**
   - Evaluate CISSP examination completion and passing score
   - Assess examination preparation and study methods
   - Review examination retake history if applicable
   - Validate examination security and integrity

4. **Endorsement Process Assessment**
   - Assess endorsement by CISSP or equivalent professional
   - Review endorsement documentation and verification
   - Validate endorser qualifications and independence
   - Confirm endorsement process compliance

5. **Continuing Professional Education (CPE)**
   - Assess 120 CPE credits earned in 3-year period
   - Review CPE activity documentation and quality
   - Validate CPE relevance to information security
   - Confirm CPE reporting accuracy and timeliness

6. **Code of Ethics Compliance**
   - Evaluate adherence to (ISC)² Code of Professional Ethics
   - Assess confidentiality and professional conduct
   - Review conflict of interest management
   - Validate ethical decision-making processes
