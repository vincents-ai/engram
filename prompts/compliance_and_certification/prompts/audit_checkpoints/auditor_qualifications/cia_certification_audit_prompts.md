## Context and Background

### CIA (Certified Internal Auditor)
**Issuing Body**: IIA (Institute of Internal Auditors)  
**Effective Date**: 1973 (ongoing certification program)  
**Scope**: Internal auditing, risk management, governance, control  
**Requirements**: Education + experience + passing exams + ethics  
**Renewal**: Every 3 years with 90 CPE credits  
**Relevance**: Required for SOX, COSO, Basel III, AML/KYC, compliance audits

## Regulatory Context
- **IIA Standards**: International standards for internal auditing
- **CPE Requirements**: Continuing professional education mandatory
- **Code of Ethics**: Professional conduct and independence requirements
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for internal audit qualifications
