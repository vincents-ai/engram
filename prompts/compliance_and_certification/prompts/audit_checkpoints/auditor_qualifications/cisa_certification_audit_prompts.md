# CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor) Certification Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for verifying CISA certification compliance and auditor qualifications for IT auditing.

## Context and Background

### CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor)
**Issuing Body**: ISACA (Information Systems Audit and Control Association)  
**Effective Date**: 1978 (ongoing certification program)  
**Scope**: IT auditing, cybersecurity, information systems governance  
**Requirements**: 5 years of experience, passing exam, adherence to Code of Ethics  
**Renewal**: Every 3 years with 120 CPE credits  
**Relevance**: Required for most IT security audits, SOC 2, ISO 27001, PCI DSS

## Regulatory Context
- **ISACA Standards**: Global standards for IT auditing and assurance
- **CPE Requirements**: Continuing professional education mandatory
- **Code of Ethics**: Professional conduct and independence requirements
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for IT audit qualifications

3. **Examination Requirements Verification**

5. **Code of Ethics Compliance**
   - Evaluate adherence to ISACA Code of Professional Ethics
   - Assess independence and objectivity in audit engagements
   - Review conflict of interest management
   - Validate professional conduct and integrity

6. **Professional Development**
