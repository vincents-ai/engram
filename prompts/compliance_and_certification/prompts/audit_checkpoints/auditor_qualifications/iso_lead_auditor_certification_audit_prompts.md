## Context and Background

### ISO Lead Auditor Certifications
**Issuing Body**: IRCA (International Register of Certificated Auditors), Exemplar Global, or equivalent  
**Effective Date**: Various (ongoing certification programs)  
**Scope**: Management system auditing, quality, environmental, health & safety, energy  
**Requirements**: Training course + examination + experience + renewal  
**Renewal**: Every 3 years with audit experience and CPD  
**Relevance**: Required for ISO 9001, ISO 14001, ISO 45001, ISO 50001 audits

1. **Certification Status Verification**
   - Evaluate ISO Lead Auditor certification validity
   - Assess certification number and registration confirmation
   - Review certification maintenance requirements
   - Validate certification renewal status and CPD

2. **Training Requirements Assessment**
   - Assess completion of accredited lead auditor training course
   - Review training provider accreditation and quality
   - Validate training content coverage and duration
   - Confirm training assessment and examination results

3. **Examination Requirements Verification**
   - Evaluate lead auditor examination completion and passing
   - Assess examination content and difficulty level
   - Review examination preparation and study methods
   - Validate examination security and integrity

4. **Experience Requirements Assessment**
   - Assess audit experience requirements (typically 2-5 years)
   - Review experience documentation and references
   - Validate experience relevance to specific ISO standards
   - Confirm experience quality and progression

5. **Continuing Professional Development (CPD)**
   - Assess CPD requirements fulfillment (varies by certification)
   - Review CPD activity documentation and quality
   - Validate CPD relevance to auditing profession
   - Confirm CPD reporting accuracy and timeliness

6. **Code of Conduct Compliance**
   - Evaluate adherence to auditor code of conduct
   - Assess independence and objectivity in audit engagements
   - Review conflict of interest management
   - Validate professional conduct and integrity
