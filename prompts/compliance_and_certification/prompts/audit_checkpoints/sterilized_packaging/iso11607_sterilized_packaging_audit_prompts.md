# ISO 11607 Sterilized Packaging Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for ISO 11607 series Packaging for Terminally Sterilized Medical Devices implementation and assessment.

## Context and Background

### ISO 11607 Series: Packaging for Terminally Sterilized Medical Devices
**Effective Date**: Various dates for different parts (2003-2019)  
**Scope**: Packaging systems for medical devices that are terminally sterilized  
**Key Principles**: Package integrity, sterility maintenance, validation, testing, documentation  
**Structure**: Multiple parts covering requirements, validation, and testing  
**Benefits**: Patient safety, product sterility assurance, regulatory compliance, quality assurance

## Regulatory Context
- **ISO 11607**: International standard for sterilized medical device packaging
- **Regulatory Requirement**: Required for terminally sterilized medical devices
- **Sterility Assurance**: Ensures package maintains product sterility
- **Global Recognition**: Accepted worldwide for packaging validation

**Packaging System Components:**

**Sterilization Compatibility:**
