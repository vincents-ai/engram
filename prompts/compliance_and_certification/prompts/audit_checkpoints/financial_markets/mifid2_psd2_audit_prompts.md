# MiFID II and PSD2 Financial Services Audit Prompts

This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for MiFID II and PSD2 financial services regulation compliance assessment.

## Context and Background

### MiFID II (Markets in Financial Instruments Directive)
**Effective Date**: January 3, 2018 (implementation date)  
**Scope**: Investment firms, trading venues, and market participants in EU  
**Key Areas**: Transparency, investor protection, market structure, reporting  
**Structure**: Directive and Regulation covering various aspects of financial markets  
**Benefits**: Enhanced market transparency, better investor protection, harmonized regulation

### PSD2 (Payment Services Directive)
**Effective Date**: January 13, 2018 (implementation date)  
**Scope**: Payment service providers and account servicing payment service providers in EU  
**Key Areas**: Open banking, payment initiation, account information, security requirements  
**Structure**: Directive covering payment services and security  
**Benefits**: Increased competition, innovation in payments, enhanced security, better consumer protection

## Regulatory Context
- **MiFID II**: EU regulation for investment services and trading venues
- **PSD2**: EU regulation for payment services and open banking
- **National Implementation**: Implemented through national laws in EU member states
- **Global Impact**: Influences financial regulation worldwide
