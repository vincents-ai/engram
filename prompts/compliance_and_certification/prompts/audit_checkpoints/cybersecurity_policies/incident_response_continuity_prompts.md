# Incident Response and Business Continuity Management Audit Checkpoint Prompts

## Overview
This collection provides comprehensive audit checkpoint prompts for incident response management per NIST SP 800-61, business continuity planning per ISO 22301, disaster recovery per NIST SP 800-34, and integrated crisis management frameworks.

---

## NIST SP 800-61 Computer Security Incident Handling

### Incident Response Preparation (Phase 1)

```
• Full-Scale Exercises: Are full-scale incident response exercises conducted periodically?

```

```
• BIA Framework: Is a comprehensive BIA framework established and implemented?

• Regulatory Impact Analysis: Are regulatory and compliance impacts analyzed?

• Customer Impact Assessment: Are customer service impacts assessed and quantified?

RECOVERY OBJECTIVES DETERMINATION:
• Recovery Time Objectives (RTO): Are RTOs established for all critical business processes?
• Recovery Point Objectives (RPO): Are RPOs established for critical data and systems?
• Minimum Business Continuity Objectives (MBCO): Are MBCOs defined for essential business functions?
• Maximum Tolerable Period of Disruption (MTPD): Are MTPDs established for business processes?
• Service Level Requirements: Are service level requirements defined for recovery scenarios?

BIA VALIDATION AND MAINTENANCE:
• BIA Accuracy Validation: Are BIA results validated through testing and stakeholder review?
• Regular Updates: Is the BIA updated regularly to reflect business changes?
• Stakeholder Review: Do business stakeholders review and approve BIA results?
• Integration with Risk Assessment: Is the BIA integrated with organizational risk assessment processes?
• Continuous Improvement: Are BIA processes continuously improved based on lessons learned?

OUTPUT REQUIREMENTS:
1. BIA methodology maturity assessment
2. Impact assessment accuracy and completeness evaluation
3. Recovery objective appropriateness analysis
4. BIA maintenance and validation effectiveness assessment
5. Specific recommendations for BIA enhancement

Include BIA templates and recommended impact assessment procedures.
```

- Information security management system prompts
- Regulatory compliance framework prompts
- Business risk management processes

---

## Usage Guidelines
1. **Risk-Based Approach**: Prioritize incident response and BC capabilities based on risk assessment
2. **Business Integration**: Ensure incident response and BC planning are integrated with business operations
3. **Regular Testing**: Implement comprehensive testing programs for all incident response and BC capabilities
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Use lessons learned from incidents and exercises for continuous improvement
5. **Stakeholder Engagement**: Ensure appropriate stakeholder engagement in incident response and BC planning

---

## Output Standards
All assessments should provide:
- Current capability maturity ratings
- Gap analysis with specific findings
- Risk-prioritized recommendations
- Implementation roadmaps with timelines
- Success metrics and monitoring procedures
- Integration points with other frameworks and business processes
