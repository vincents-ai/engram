## CSRD Requirements Audited

### Article 19a: Sustainability Reporting

#### Control: ESG Data Collection and Reporting

**Status**: ⚠️ **OBSERVATION**

**Requirements Checked**:
- Environmental, social, and governance data collection
- EU Taxonomy alignment assessment
- Double materiality assessment completed

**Evidence Examined**:
- `esg/data-collection/environmental-metrics-2024.xlsx`
- `esg/taxonomy/eu-taxonomy-assessment-2024.pdf`
- `governance/materiality/double-materiality-assessment-2024.pdf`

**Findings**:
- Environmental data collection in place for Scope 1 & 2 emissions
- Scope 3 emissions data incomplete (supply chain gaps)
- EU Taxonomy assessment completed but alignment scoring methodology needs refinement
- Double materiality assessment conducted with stakeholder engagement

**Conclusion**: Reporting framework established but data quality needs improvement.

### Article 29b: Digital Reporting Format

#### Control: Machine-Readable XBRL Reporting

**Status**: ⚠️ **OBSERVATION**

**Requirements Checked**:
- XBRL taxonomy implementation for sustainability data
- Digital reporting infrastructure
- Data validation and quality controls

**Evidence Examined**:
- `esg/reporting/xbrl-implementation-plan.md`
- `systems/esg-reporting/data-pipeline-architecture.md`
- `compliance/csrd/digital-reporting-readiness.xlsx`

**Findings**:
